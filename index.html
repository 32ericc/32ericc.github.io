About

Hi, I'm Eric — a student 👨‍🎓 based in Austin, Texas, currently pursuing a BS in Electrical Engineering at Texas A&M University (TAMU)👍.

Up until last year, I had zero programming experience, but I have since become a self-taught programmer, driven by the fact that I have an affinity for the complexities and depth of specializations in the world of tech (I also had nothing better to do 😅). To me, this website marks my first notable project, and I am proud to say that I managed to build it by sourcing free videos and articles from the web 🌐.

Outside of tech-related stuff, I am extremely passionate about perpetual learning 📚, discovering new music 🎶, maintaining a fitness routine ⛹️‍♂️, and delving into some form of art 🎨. Photography 📸 is also a newfound interest for me.

I don't necessarily identify myself with a specific field; instead, I am in the process of exploring different options and seeing where it leads me. The journey has its obstacles, but I am excited to see the opportunities that await 🛣️.
